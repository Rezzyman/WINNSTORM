## 1. PURPOSE

The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship concerning **damage assessment technology, inspection methodologies, software platforms, and related services** (the "Purpose"). In connection with the Purpose, each Party may disclose to the other certain confidential technical and business information that the disclosing Party desires the receiving Party to treat as confidential.

---

## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

**"Confidential Information"** means any and all non-public information, in any form, furnished or made available directly or indirectly by one Party to the other, including but not limited to:

### 2.1 Technical Information
- Software source code, algorithms, and architectures
- The Winn Methodology™ and related inspection processes
- AI/ML models, training data, and analytical frameworks
- API specifications, database schemas, and system designs
- Mobile application designs and functionality
- Thermal imaging analysis techniques
- Weather verification systems and data sources

### 2.2 Business Information
- Business plans, strategies, and financial information
- Customer and client lists, including property data
- Pricing structures and subscription models
- Marketing strategies and sales pipelines
- Partnership and vendor relationships
- Employee and contractor information

### 2.3 Exclusions
Confidential Information does not include information that:
- (a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving Party;
- (b) Was rightfully in the receiving Party's possession prior to disclosure;
- (c) Is rightfully obtained by the receiving Party from a third party without restriction;
- (d) Is independently developed by the receiving Party without use of Confidential Information;
- (e) Is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or court order (with prompt notice to the disclosing Party).

---

## 3. OBLIGATIONS OF RECEIVING PARTY

The receiving Party agrees to:

### 3.1 Confidentiality
- Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence
- Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent
- Use at least the same degree of care to protect Confidential Information as it uses to protect its own confidential information, but in no event less than reasonable care

### 3.2 Limited Use
- Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose
- Not use Confidential Information for any other purpose, including competitive analysis
- Not reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble any software or technology disclosed

### 3.3 Limited Disclosure
- Limit disclosure to employees, contractors, and advisors who:
  - Have a need to know for the Purpose
  - Are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as this Agreement
- Be responsible for any breach by such persons

## 5. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 5.1 Term
This Agreement shall remain in effect for **two (2) years** from the Effective Date, unless terminated earlier by either Party with thirty (30) days' written notice.

### 5.2 Survival
The confidentiality obligations herein shall survive termination and continue for a period of **three (3) years** following termination, or indefinitely for trade secrets as defined under applicable law.

### 5.3 Return of Materials
Upon termination or request, the receiving Party shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information and certify such destruction in writing.
