## AGREEMENT TERMS

### 1. CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS

Each Party agrees to:

1.1 Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence and not disclose it to any third party without prior written consent.

1.2 Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose stated above.

1.3 Limit internal disclosure to employees and contractors with a need to know who are bound by confidentiality obligations.

1.4 Use at least the same degree of care to protect Confidential Information as used for its own confidential information, but not less than reasonable care.

1.5 Not reverse engineer, decompile, or derive source code from any disclosed technology.

### 2. EXCLUSIONS

This Agreement does not apply to information that:
- Is or becomes publicly available without breach
- Was known to the receiving Party before disclosure
- Is independently developed without use of Confidential Information
- Is obtained from a third party without restriction
- Must be disclosed by law (with prompt notice)
